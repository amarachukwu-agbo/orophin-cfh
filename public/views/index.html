About Game

Cards for Humanity is a fast-paced online version of the popular card game,
Cards Against Humanity, that gives you the opportinity to donate to children in need -
all while remaining as despicable and awkward as you naturally are.

Warning! This game contains offensive, profane, explicit content created by Cards Against Humanity and provided here,
uncensored and complete, in digital form. Human Resources made us say that.


How To Play

Charity

"If you don't give back, no one will like you™" - crowdrise.com

We're quite sure you feel the same way, so we've integrated donation widgets (via crowdrise.com), along with some not-so-subtle reminders throughout the game to give you the opportunity to donate. If you're signed in when you donate, we'll even upgrade your account and place a crown on your avatar's head (to remind other players how much better you are than them). All donations go to the Make-a-Wish Foundation and benefit children in need.

Legal stuff: This game is based on the Cards Against Humanity card game, which is CC licensed (BY-NC-SA). Cards for Humanity is not affiliated with Cards Against Humanity. In complying with the Creative Commons license of the Cards Against Humanity card game, all proceeds from donations go directly to charity, as managed by crowdrise.
